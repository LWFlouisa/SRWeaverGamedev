Ehena's Silent Lunch

  1. Ehena-Marie, girlfriend of Hemato-Tomato, moved to the US from France with her father, leaving behind her sisters whom are grown adults by this point.

  • The fact that she is only half French is a source of anxiety and self-esteem issues, compounding by her lack of fluency in Japanese despite her mom being Japanese.

  • She introduces herself to the class by speaking in her native French. She presents a brave facade despite being an emotional wreck, but at this point we don't know her troubles.

  1. Ehena's father is extremely traditional, almost to a fault. While other students could wear largely what they wanted, she was stuck having to be created with how she wore 1950s style dresses, despite the fact that it was the year 2016, three years before she was executed,

  • Her father would mostly watch old western movies, and barely pay attention to his daughter.

  • Her father cares more about her mother than her, which is a source oc self-esteem issues. This was before her visions and hallucinations began, if they are hallucinations.

  1. Ehena was not really sure whether her girlfriend liked her for her or her footwear choices, as she was more the type of girl to wear Birkenstock boston clogs and Steel Toe boots, while others in more formal occassions wearing saddle shoes.
  1. Ehena describes herself as tall as a short male, which makes her somewhat taller than most girls her age. This made it difficult to find clothes that properly fit, especially in more traditional clothes like 1950s school uniforms.
  1. While she describes herself as a conformist, in truth she's closer to passive aggressively counter-cultural. She takes on the appearance of conformity in order to please her father, but when not have her real nature begins to surface.
  1. Her weapon of choice is Arsenic and Daggers, although in practice do to the nature of her father's conservative house, this weirdly meant that a dagger was more likely to not be detected in contrast with poison.

  • When she went to quarries with Hemato and her best guy friend, this meant that she could only study how to get Arsenic when visiting a Hemato's best friend's house.

  • Until one day when she decided that she no longer wanted her father in the picture, but she had not yet decided she wanted to be rid of him altogether.

  1. In the school Hemato and Ehena went to, the school allowed for capital punishment by guillotine for those whom broke silence rules during lunch time. Although at first this is assumed to be a rumor, patially corroborated by the fact that no student sent to the office has ever returned. By there is an infamous room known as "Salle De Mort", the halls of death.

  • This is where we find out that Ehena-Marie might not be the most reliable narrator, as she describes students as being taken by security guards to the Salle De Mort.

  • Silent lunch had been a gradual increase of strictness ever sense the French take over of the United States after World War III. How much it based on the actual stricrtness of the school staff or Ehena's poor reliability is not clear.

  1. Hemato-Tomato is aware of Ehena's feelings about her father, but opt to not report her to the authority, whom have grown increasingly tyrannical in recent months.

  • Technically Hemato-Tomato could have ratted her out at any point, and there are indications she may know about the monster in which Ehena Marie sees in her bedroom.

  • Hemato taunts Ehena by showing her the severed head of one of her female classmates, which all but confirms that indeed students whom violent silent lunch get the ultimate penalty: being beheaded by guillotine.

  1. At bedtime after her father has went to bed, she would see this bear-like creature in her closet that came from hell, and had vampire like tendencies.

  • This bear-bat, the bear-bat of ghouls, acts as a grim repear to transfer souls to the afterlife, with his henchmen being ursinepires.

  • An Ursinepiros is a vampire bear-like person whom came back from the dead to have vengance on thos whom had killed them in battle during life.

  • Eventually she finds her that her sister had become one of these beasts, and she had traveled to the US to see her sister for one last time.

  • Eventually the bear-bat offers to murder Ehena's father, in exchange for her soul. Although this is uncertain what impact this would be given that reincarnation is an esstablished thing in this universe.

    • Eventually this culminates into her murdering her father, by slitting his throat with one of her daggers, then locking him in a chest in her attic.

      • She woke up her bed with leaves in it, as if she had been previously out in the midnight forest.

        • Hemato eventually swears revenge against the government that executed the only girl she had ever loved more than anyone else.

      • Eventually she is arrested when a S.W.A.T team was sent to her house under suspicions of comitting murder.

  • These creatures take on a human like form in silhouette, except during thefull moon when they change into this vampiric bear-like beast.

  1. It wasn't unheard for characters in sitcoms in this alternate history to be wiped from official records, meaning that wasn't really a way to know for sure whether a character actually existed. This caused cognitive dissonance when she thought she remembered a grandma figure in a sitcome she used to watch.

  • Censorship in general had gotten more strict ever sense the French take over of the United States.

  • This harsh censorship never totally left during the Franco-Japanese wars, that eventually culminated in the partitioning of the US into Japanese states and French states.
