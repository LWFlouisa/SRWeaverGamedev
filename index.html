What Is Chronomoire

"Chronomoire - An offshoot of Cyberpunk that blends High Tech Low Life aspects of Cyberpunk, and Neo-Belle Epoque elemnts in plots around temporal displacement. Such protagonists are often societal outcasts, such as serial murderesses fleeing asylums. With "District 1830" being specific historical sub-divisions of an otherwise Cybernetic and Digital societal framework. With core conflicts between social conservative values and modern progress. In a sense, Belle Epoque merges into the present.

Yumemoire is a specific subset of Chronomoire or Chronopunk that emphasizes dream-logic and psychic espionage, and often blend Isekai elements."
