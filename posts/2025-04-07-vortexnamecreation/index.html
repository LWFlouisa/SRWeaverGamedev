Vortex Based Name Creation

This video introduces the concept of vortex based name creation, which is a branch from procedural name generation for Roguelike games. Here the idea is to create names for individual members of enemy units rather than singular enemies. Mostly this is applicable for newly creation names rather than enemies whom already have names.
